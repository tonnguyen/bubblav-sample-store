# Returns & Refunds

At VELVET & VOGUE, we want you to love everything you buy. If something isn't quite right, our hassle-free return policy makes it easy to exchange or return your items.

---

## 30-Day Return Policy

You have **30 days** from the date of delivery to return items for a full refund or exchange.

### Return Conditions
- Items must be unworn, unwashed, and in original condition
- Original tags must still be attached
- Items must be returned in original packaging
- Proof of purchase required

### Non-Returnable Items
- Final sale items (marked as "Final Sale")
- Underwear and swimwear (for hygiene reasons)
- Personalized or custom-made items
- Piercing jewelry (for hygiene reasons)
- Gift cards

---

## How to Return an Item

### Option 1: Return via Service Point (Recommended)

1. **Initiate Your Return**
   - Log into your account or visit [Returns Portal](/returns)
   - Enter your order number and email
   - Select the items you want to return
   - Choose refund or exchange

2. **Print Your Return Label**
   - Download and print the prepaid return label
   - Attach it to your package

3. **Drop Off Your Package**
   - Take your package to any PostNord service point
   - Keep your receipt as proof of return

4. **Receive Your Refund**
   - Refunds are processed within 5-7 business days after we receive your return
   - You'll receive an email confirmation when processed

### Option 2: In-Store Return

Visit our Stockholm boutique with:
- Your item(s) in original condition
- Order confirmation or receipt
- Valid ID

We'll process your return or exchange immediately.

## Exchanges

We're happy to exchange your item for a different size or color, subject to availability.

### Exchange Process
1. Initiate return via [Returns Portal](/returns)
2. Select "Exchange" and choose your preferred size/color
3. We'll ship your new item once we receive the original
4. No additional shipping fee for exchanges

### If Your Exchange Item is Unavailable
If your preferred item is out of stock, we'll:
- Offer a refund immediately
- Notify you when it's back in stock
- Suggest similar items

## Refunds

### Refund Method
Refunds are issued to your original payment method:
- **Credit/Debit Card**: 5-7 business days
- **PayPal**: 3-5 business days
- **Klarna**: Processed according to Klarna's timeline
- **Gift Card**: Issued immediately as store credit

### Partial Refunds
If you return some items from a multi-item order, you'll be refunded for the returned items only. Shipping costs are non-refundable unless the return is due to our error.

---

## Damaged or Defective Items

Received a damaged or defective item? We're sorry! Contact us immediately:

1. Email support@velvetandvogue.com with:
   - Your order number
   - Photos of the damage/defect
   - Description of the issue

2. We'll arrange:
   - Free return shipping
   - Full refund or replacement at no cost
   - Priority processing

---

## Late or Lost Returns

If your return hasn't reached us after 14 days:
1. Contact us with your tracking number
2. We'll investigate with the carrier
3. If lost, we'll issue a full refund or replacement

---

## Return Policy Exceptions

### Holiday Extended Returns
During the holiday season, we extend our return window:
- **November-December purchases**: Returns accepted until January 31st

### Final Sale Items
Items marked "Final Sale" cannot be returned or exchanged. These are clearly marked on the product page and at checkout.

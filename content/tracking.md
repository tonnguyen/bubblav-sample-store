## Understanding Your Order Status

### Order Placed
Your order has been received and is being processed.
- **Next Step**: Order confirmation email sent
- **Timeline**: Immediate

### Processing
We're preparing your order for shipment.
- **Next Step**: Quality check and packaging
- **Timeline**: 1-2 business days

### Shipped
Your order has left our warehouse and is on its way!
- **Next Step**: In transit to delivery address
- **Timeline**: Depends on shipping method selected

### Out for Delivery
Your package is with the local carrier and will be delivered today.
- **Next Step**: Delivery to your address or service point
- **Timeline**: Same day

### Delivered
Your package has been delivered.
- **Next Step**: Enjoy your new VELVET & VOGUE pieces!

## Delivery Stages Explained

### Stage 1: Order Processing (1-2 business days)
Once you place your order:
- We confirm payment
- We verify inventory
- We pick and pack your items
- We generate your shipping label

### Stage 2: Carrier Pickup (Same day or next business day)
- Your package is handed to the carrier (PostNord/DB Schenker)
- Tracking information becomes active
- You'll receive an email with tracking number

### Stage 3: In Transit
- Your package travels through the carrier's network
- May pass through distribution centers
- Tracking updates as it moves closer to you

### Stage 4: Local Delivery
- Package arrives at your local distribution center
- Scheduled for delivery to your address
- Or available for pickup at service point

### Stage 5: Delivery
- Package delivered to your door
- Or available for collection at service point
- Delivery confirmation sent via email

## Delivery Instructions

### Home Delivery
- Ensure someone is available to receive the package
- If not home, carrier may leave at doorstep or with neighbor
- Package may be taken to service point after failed delivery

### Service Point Pickup
- Packages held for 14 days
- Bring ID and tracking number
- Pickup anytime during service point opening hours

### Delivery Address Instructions
Add special instructions at checkout:
- "Leave at doorstep"
- "Ring doorbell"
- "Deliver to neighbor"
- "Leave in designated box"

## Tracking Issues?

### Tracking Not Updating
If tracking hasn't updated in 24-48 hours:
- Allow time for package to reach next scanning location
- Check back later – updates may be delayed
- Contact us if no updates after 48 hours

### Tracking Number Not Found
- Double-check your tracking number for typos
- Wait 2-4 hours after receiving shipping confirmation
- Contact us if issue persists

### Delayed Delivery
Your package may be delayed due to:
- High volume periods (holidays, sales)
- Weather conditions
- Carrier operational issues

We'll notify you of significant delays affecting your order.

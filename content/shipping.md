# Shipping & Delivery

We want you to enjoy your VELVET & VOGUE pieces as soon as possible. Here's everything you need to know about our shipping options and delivery times.

---

## Domestic Shipping (Sweden)

### Standard Delivery – FREE on orders over 500 SEK
- **Delivery Time**: 2-4 business days
- **Cost**: 49 SEK (orders under 500 SEK)
- **Carrier**: PostNord
- **Tracking**: Included

### Express Delivery
- **Delivery Time**: 1-2 business days
- **Cost**: 99 SEK
- **Carrier**: DB Schenker
- **Tracking**: Real-time updates

---

## International Shipping

We ship to most European countries. Shipping rates and delivery times vary by destination.

### Europe (EU)
| Destination | Standard Delivery | Express Delivery |
|-------------|-------------------|------------------|
| Denmark, Norway, Finland | 3-5 days (79 SEK) | 2-3 days (149 SEK) |
| Germany, Netherlands, Belgium | 4-6 days (99 SEK) | 2-3 days (169 SEK) |
| France, Italy, Spain | 5-7 days (129 SEK) | 3-4 days (199 SEK) |
| Rest of EU | 6-8 days (149 SEK) | 4-5 days (229 SEK) |

### Outside EU
- **United Kingdom**: 5-7 days (149 SEK)
- **Switzerland**: 5-7 days (149 SEK)
- **Other countries**: Contact us for rates

---

## Order Processing

- **Processing Time**: Orders are processed within 24-48 hours on business days
- **Cut-off Time**: Orders placed before 2 PM CET are shipped the same day
- **Weekend Orders**: Orders placed Friday-Sunday are processed on Monday

---

## Tracking Your Order

Once your order ships, you'll receive an email with your tracking number. Track your package anytime:

1. Visit our [Order Tracking](/tracking) page
2. Enter your order number and email
3. Get real-time delivery updates

## Customs & Import Duties (International Orders)

- **EU Customers**: No additional customs or import duties
- **Non-EU Customers**: You may be responsible for customs fees, import duties, and taxes
- These charges are not included in our pricing and are collected by customs at delivery

---

## Shipping to Multiple Addresses

Want to send gifts to different addresses? We've got you covered:

1. Place separate orders for each address
2. Or contact us – we can arrange split shipments for an additional fee

---

## Failed Delivery Attempts

If delivery fails:
- The carrier will leave a notification with instructions
- You can reschedule delivery or pick up at a service point
- After 2 failed attempts, the package will be returned to us
- Return shipping fees may apply

---

## Weather Delays

Severe weather may impact delivery times. We'll notify you of significant delays affecting your order.

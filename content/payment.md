# Payment Methods

We offer a variety of secure payment options to make your shopping experience convenient and safe. All transactions are encrypted and processed with the highest security standards.

---

## Accepted Payment Methods

### Credit & Debit Cards
We accept all major cards:
- Visa
- Mastercard
- American Express
- Maestro

**Processing**: Secure SSL encryption via Stripe
**No additional fees**

### PayPal
Shop quickly and securely using your PayPal account:
- **Checkout**: Select PayPal at checkout
- **No account required**: Pay with card via PayPal guest checkout
- **Buyer Protection**: Eligible purchases covered by PayPal's protection program

### Klarna – Buy Now, Pay Later
Shop now and pay later with Klarna. Available for Swedish residents:

#### Klarna Checkout
- Pay within 14 days – interest-free
- No hidden fees
- Try before you buy – pay only for what you keep

#### Klarna Installments
- Split your purchase into 3 monthly payments
- Interest-free on orders over 500 SEK
- Flexible payment options

#### Klarna Financing
- Monthly installments up to 36 months
- Credit decision in seconds
- Transparent interest rates

### Apple Pay & Google Pay
Quick, secure checkout with your digital wallet:
- **Fast checkout**: No need to enter card details
- **Secure**: Your card information is never shared
- **Available**: On compatible devices and browsers

### Swish (Swedish Customers)
Pay directly with your mobile:
- **Instant**: Payment processed immediately
- **Secure**: Mobile BankID authentication
- **No extra fees**: Just like regular online banking

## Payment Issues

### Declined Payments
If your payment is declined:
- Check your card details are correct
- Ensure sufficient funds are available
- Try a different payment method
- Contact your bank if the issue persists

### Pending Payments
Some payments may appear as "pending" before processing:
- This is normal – funds are reserved but not charged
- Pending status typically clears within 1-3 business days
- You'll receive confirmation once payment is processed

### Refund Processing
Refund timing varies by payment method:
- **Credit/Debit Cards**: 5-7 business days
- **PayPal**: 3-5 business days
- **Klarna**: Processed according to Klarna's timeline
- **Swish**: Immediate

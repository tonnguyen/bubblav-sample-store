## How to Measure

For accurate measurements, measure directly on your body or over thin clothing.

### Bust
- Measure around the fullest part of your chest
- Keep the tape measure level and comfortable
- Arms should be relaxed at your sides

### Waist
- Measure around the narrowest part of your waist
- Usually just above the belly button
- Keep the tape measure snug but not tight

### Hips
- Measure around the fullest part of your hips
- Keep feet together
- Tape measure should be level

### Inseam
- Measure from the crotch to the ankle
- For best results, measure a pair of pants that fit well

### Sleeve Length
- Measure from the center of your back to the wrist
- Arm should be slightly bent

## Fit Guide

### Slim Fit
- Close to the body
- Follows body contours
- Modern, tailored look
- Size up for a more relaxed fit

### Regular Fit
- Classic fit
- Comfortable without being loose
- True to size
- Most versatile option

### Relaxed Fit
- Looser fit
- Comfortable and casual
- Size down for a more fitted look

### Oversized
- Intentionally loose
- Trendy, modern silhouette
- Size down for a less oversized look

## Product-Specific Notes

### Denim
- Denim may stretch slightly with wear
- Consider sizing down if between sizes
- Pre-washed denim holds shape better

### Knitwear
- Natural fibers may relax over time
- Size down if you prefer a fitted look
- Cashmere: handle with care, may pill initially

### Outerwear
- Consider layering underneath
- Size up for wearing over thick sweaters
- Check product-specific measurements

### Swimwear
- True to size
- Consider coverage preferences
- Check specific product measurements

## Size Tips

### Between Sizes?
- **Tops**: Size up for a more relaxed fit
- **Bottoms**: Size down if you have a smaller waist
- **Shoes**: Size up for wider feet

### Height Considerations
- **Petite (under 160cm)**: XS-S sizes may work best
- **Regular (160-170cm)**: Standard sizing should fit
- **Tall (170cm+)**: Consider longer inseam options

### Body Type
- **Pear shape**: Size up for bottoms to accommodate hips
- **Apple shape**: Size up for tops to accommodate waist
- **Athletic build**: Consider regular or relaxed fit options
